Simon Richter

Simon, a paradox: a man feared in the criminal underworld yet controlled by discipline and drill.

A powerful yet shadow like figure in the European underworld, Simon Richter is both a brutal strategist and a scarred individual.
Born into hardship, forged in war and destroyed by betrayal, he has become what his callsign always alluded to: a shadow.
His empire, built on blood, power and his endless desire for dominance now stretching across Europe and beyond its borders, running behind the facade of legitimacy his companies grant.

Yet beneath the surface, ghosts of his past continue to haunt him: reminders of what he has lost and maybe, what he still has left to lose.

Simon Richter—known as "Schatten"—is a man shaped by relentless hardship. From a childhood in Munich’s impoverished neighborhoods to an illustrious yet brutal military career in the Bundeswehr and KSK, every mark on his body narrates a tale of survival and retribution. Today, he wields absolute power in the criminal underworld with calculated ruthlessness and an iron will.

Background & Story

Born and raised in the harsh neighborhoods of Munich, Simon’s early life was marked by poverty and strict discipline. Under the weight of familial expectations and a brutal environment, he quickly learned to be resourceful and determined—traits that would serve him well in later years.

Enlisting in the Bundeswehr, Simon’s exceptional aptitude led him to join the elite Kommando Spezialkräfte (KSK). His military tenure took him across global conflict zones where he earned both respect and scars. A turning point came when he was captured and brutally tortured by a crime syndicate—a trauma that not only shattered his faith in authority but also sparked his relentless quest for power and vengeance.

Today, Simon reigns over one of Europe’s most formidable criminal empires. His front, the Richter Holdings Group, masks an intricate network of drug trafficking, arms dealing, money laundering, and other covert operations. Every decision is driven by military precision and an unyielding desire for control, making him both a master strategist and a feared underworld figure.

Balancing brutal enforcement with a veneer of sophisticated legitimacy, Simon’s empire is a testament to his ability to transform pain into power.
